About Me

I'm Cody Brookes, your professional Cloud Bouncer and Access Control Specialist. My job is simple: ensuring that in the digital world, only the right people get the right keys to the right doors—nothing more.

At Fidelity Investments, I laid down the law on access, specializing in resolving complex identity and governance issues and architecting provisioning workflows across massive enterprise systems.

I don't just set rules; I automate their enforcement. I've built custom serverless solutions (AWS Lambda/n8n/Power Automate) and monitoring stacks to enforce Zero Trust and least privilege principles from the start.

My current focus is mastering secure environments built from the ground up using Infrastructure as Code (IaC) across AWS and Azure, integrating robust security policies directly into the DevOps pipeline.

When I’m not playing digital bouncer, you can find me hiking or playing basketball (I even played at the college level!). I guess I just like locking down the defense, both on and off the court!
