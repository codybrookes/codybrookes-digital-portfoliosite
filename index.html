About Me

Hey there! I'm Cody Brookes, and I'm essentially your professional Cloud Bouncer and Access Control Specialist.

I spend my time ensuring that in the sprawling digital world of cloud infrastructure, only the right people get the right keys to the right doors—and absolutely nothing more. I operate at the intersection of **security**, **governance**, and **cloud architecture**.

My background at Fidelity Investments wasn't just about fixing things; it was about laying down the law and protecting the digital perimeter. As a Technology Support Lead and Operations Consultant, I was responsible for resolving complex **identity and access issues**, architecting **governance policies**, and **streamlining provisioning workflows** across enormous enterprise systems.

I don't just set rules; I automate their enforcement. I've built tools like a full-stack EC2 Monitoring and Audit Stack and deployed custom serverless access request automations (using AWS Lambda and low-code platforms like Power Automate and n8n). My core mission is integrating **least privilege** and **Zero Trust** principles into every project.

Currently, my focus is mastering how to build secure environments from the ground up using Infrastructure as Code (IaC) across AWS and Azure. I’m all about integrating robust **security policies** directly into the **DevOps pipeline**—security first, always.

When I’m not playing digital bouncer, you can find me exploring new places, hiking, or playing basketball (I even played at the college level!). I guess I just like locking down the defense, both on and off the court!
